COmprehensive Boat
Inspections 

Each consignment boat receives an 8-hour full marine inspection performed by one of our certified technicians. This includes: 

  • Full diagnostic and compression testing 
  • Fluid testing and replacement
  • Electrical and mechanical system checks
  • Lake test and performance review 

These steps give your boat a clear edge on the open market and help buyers feel confident they’re investing in a quality vessel. Plus, Tobler lists your boat not only locally but also on major national platforms like Boat Trader, OnlyInboards, and OnlyOutboards to maximize exposure and reach motivated buyers across the country.

What types of boats can I consign with Tobler Marina?

We accept a variety of pre-owned boats including wake, surf, pontoon, and fishing models.

What is included in the 8-hour inspection?

Our certified technicians perform a full diagnostic, fluid replacement, compression test, and lake test to verify your boat’s condition and performance.

How long does it take to sell my boat?

Timing depends on market demand and season, but most boats sell faster when they’ve completed our inspection process, are listed on multiple national boat listing platforms and are priced competitively.

Do I need to be present during the inspection?

No — we’ll handle the entire process and keep you updated with detailed reports and photos.

Can Tobler buy my boat outright instead of consigning?

Yes. We offer direct purchases for those who prefer to sell quickly without waiting for a consignment sale.
