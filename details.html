What types of boats do you detail?

We detail all kinds - surf, pontoon, wake, and fishing boats. If it floats, we can clean it.

How often should I have my boat detailed?

Twice a year is ideal: once before summer and again before winter. It keeps your boat protected and always ready to go.

Do you handle scratches or gel coat touch-ups?

Yes! We can repair light scratches, chips, and oxidation to bring your finish back to smooth and shiny.

Can I drop my boat off anytime?

Give us a call first, we’ll find a time that works for your schedule and get you right in.

Do you use safe products for Idaho lakes?

Always. We only use high-quality, eco-friendly products designed for marine use that protect both your boat and our waterways.
