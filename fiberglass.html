FAQ

What types of fiberglass damage can you repair?

We handle everything from minor surface cracks and chips to full transom or hull repairs.

Can you color-match my boat’s gelcoat?

Yes! Our technicians use precision color-matching tools to blend new gelcoat perfectly with your boat’s existing finish.

How long does fiberglass repair take?

Smaller repairs can often be completed in a few days, while full restorations or structural jobs may take several weeks depending on the scope.

Do you repair oxidation or faded finishes?

Absolutely. Many customers pair summerization with our professional detailing, fiberglass repair, or valet transport to make boating season even easier.

Is fiberglass repair covered by insurance?

In many cases, yes. We work with major insurance providers and can help coordinate the claims process for covered damage.
