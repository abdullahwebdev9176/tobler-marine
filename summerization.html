Comprehensive
Summerization
Services 

Whether your boat has been in storage or just needs a seasonal tune-up, our team will handle every key system to ensure it’s launch-ready and worry-free.
Our summerization process includes: 

  • Battery testing and charging   
  • Fluid checks and top-offs   
  • Reinstalling plugs and hoses   
  • Bilge and blower operation testing   
  • Engine warm-up to operating temperature   
  • Safety and trailer light inspection   
  • Ballast, heater, and shower system reconnections (where applicable)  
  • Complimentary service clean for boats stored with us   

Optional upgrades include raw water impeller replacement, oil and filter changes, gear lube replacement, and additional maintenance as recommended.

FAQ

What is summerization or spring recommission?

It’s the process of getting your boat ready for the season — inspecting, testing, and reconnecting all major systems to ensure safe and reliable operation after winter storage.

When should I schedule summerization?

We recommend scheduling by early spring to avoid the seasonal rush. Our calendar fills quickly once temperatures rise.

Do you offer pricing online?

Yes! We list all pricing and service details online so you always know what to expect before booking.

Can I combine summerization with detailing or valet service?

Absolutely. Many customers pair summerization with our professional detailing, fiberglass repair, or valet transport to make boating season even easier.

Is impeller replacement included?

Impeller replacement is available as an add-on and is highly recommended once per year to protect your engine from overheating.
