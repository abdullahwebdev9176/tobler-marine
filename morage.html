Rivers Edge Marina 

Located in Post Falls, Rivers Edge Marina offers convenient access to the Spokane River and Lake Coeur d’Alene, an excellent choice for families, jet ski owners, and weekend boaters. Only 9 miles from the marina to downtown Coeur d’Alene, a 10–20 minute boat ride depending on river traffic making Rivers Edge a quiet alternative to dockage on the lake. Skip the crowds and enjoy proximity to the same lakefront dining, shopping, and recreation without the congestion or resort markup.  

Slip Sizes & Access

Slip sizes include 20 ft, 22 ft, 24 ft, 30 ft, and jet ski spaces, perfect for a wide range of boats and personal watercraft. We also offer one 35 foot side tie slip. Enjoy quick access to both the Spokane River and Lake Coeur d’Alene for day trips, surf sessions, or evening cruises.  

Marina Features & Amenities  

Enjoy full-service amenities designed for comfort and convenience:    

  • Fuel dock & convenience store with snacks, refreshments, and boating essentials  
  • Shore power and storage lockers  
  • Secure, gated dock access
  • Dedicated parking  
  • Dock boxes with power included for each slip  
  • 24/7 access for boaters   
  • Jet ski slips and moorage available  
  • Across from Q’emlin Park in Post Falls, Idaho   
  • Friendly, community atmosphere   

Seasonal Availability 

Rivers Edge operates seasonally, with availability based on the Avista dam spillway gate operation. Slips typically open in mid-June and close in late October. Contact our team to check current openings and reserve your preferred slip size.   

Transient Moorage   

Transient moorage is available on a weekly basis (booked one week at a time, up to two consecutive weeks). It’s the perfect option for vacationers, weekend visitors, or boaters looking for temporary access to the Spokane River and Lake Coeur d’Alene.

FAQ

Where is Rivers Edge Marina located?

Rivers Edge Marina is located in Post Falls, Idaho, along the Spokane River — about 9 miles and a short 10–20 minute boat ride from downtown Coeur d’Alene and the CDA Resort.

Do you offer transient moorage?

Yes. Transient moorage is available weekly for boats and jet skis, with flexible scheduling options throughout the season.

What amenities are available at the marina?

Rivers Edge offers fuel service, shore power, dock boxes, dedicated parking, jet ski slips, and a convenience store stocked with snacks and boating essentials.

When does the moorage season run?

The marina typically opens mid-June and closes in late October, depending on spillway gate operations and water levels.

Can I reserve a slip in advance?

Yes. Submit a moorage request form to the Tobler Marina team to check availability and secure your preferred slip size for the season or short-term stay.
