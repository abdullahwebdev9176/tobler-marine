Whats Included

Our valet service is designed to make boat ownership effortless, handling every detail so you can spend less time coordinating and more time enjoying your days on the water:

  • Safe pickup and transport between storage and launch 
  • Dockside delivery when you’re ready to go 
  • Post-use rinse and secure storage 
  • Optional fuel-up and maintenance check 
  • Coordination with our Service and Detailing teams for ongoing care 

Why Boaters Love It

FAQ

What is boat valet service?

Our valet service handles all the logistics of boating — launching, retrieval, transport, and storage — so you can simply show up and enjoy your day.

Do you offer boat delivery to the dock?

Yes. We’ll have your boat launched and ready at the dock when you arrive, and we’ll secure it after you’re done.

Can you combine valet with winterization or detailing?

Absolutely. We can coordinate valet service alongside maintenance, detailing, or seasonal storage.

How do I schedule valet service?

Just call our service team or submit a request online. We’ll confirm your pickup time and handle the rest.

Is valet available year-round?

Valet is available seasonally and during select off-season appointments — ask our team for current scheduling options.
