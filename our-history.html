OUR HISTORY

For over 75 years, Tobler Marina has been proudly serving North Idaho boaters with dedication and heart. We’ve built our legacy on local roots, lasting relationships, and a shared love for life on the water.

On the Water
since 1948

First established in 1948, Tobler Marina is the oldest certified dealer and one of the largest boat dealerships serving the Inland Northwest. What began as a small, family-run business has grown into a trusted name in the region’s boating community.

North Idaho
Roots

With three locations, we are proud to support our local community as a family owned and operated dealership. Tobler Marina has always been about connection between people, their boats, and the water. Over the decades, we have built lasting relationships with boaters who share our love for life on the lake.

Built on History.

Today, Tobler Marina continues to provide full-service boating experiences from sales and service to storage, detailing, and year-round support. Whether you are buying your first boat or maintaining a family favorite, our goal is to make every step easy and enjoyable.
