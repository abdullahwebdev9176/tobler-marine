What types of boats fit in Tobler’s indoor storage?

We can accommodate most boat styles including wake, surf, pontoon, and fishing boats. Contact us for size and height clearance details.

Is trailer storage available year-round?

Yes. Our trailer storage is open throughout the year. Perfect for freeing up space at home or protecting your trailer during the winter.

Do you offer heated or climate-controlled storage?

Yes. Our indoor storage facility provides temperature-stable conditions to prevent freezing and moisture damage.

Can I schedule service before pickup?

Absolutely. Many customers pair storage with spring maintenance or detailing. Just let us know when you’d like your boat prepped for pickup.

How early should I reserve storage space?

We recommend reserving early in the fall, as indoor storage space fills quickly before the first freeze.
