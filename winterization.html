FAQ

Why is winterizing my boat important?

Winterizing prevents freeze damage to your engine, fuel, and water systems saving you from costly repairs when spring arrives.

What’s included in your winterization service?

Our full winter service includes engine prep, draining and flushing water lines, fuel stabilization, fluid replacement, and battery care all performed by factory-certified technicians.

When should I book my winterization?

We recommend scheduling by early fall, before overnight temperatures dip below freezing. Appointments fill quickly, so reserve early for your ideal time slot.

Can you store my boat after winterization?

Yes. We offer secure indoor and outdoor winter storage right here in Hayden, along with trailer storage options.

Do you provide pick-up or valet service?

Yes, Tobler offers valet-style transport for select service and storage customers. Ask our team for current availability when booking.
