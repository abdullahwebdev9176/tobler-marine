What types of canvas and upholstery services do you offer?

We provide full-service canvas and upholstery work including enclosures, mooring covers, travel covers, seat repair, and custom stitching.

Can you match my boat’s original colors or design?

Yes. Our team can match existing fabrics, textures, and colors, or help you select new materials for a refreshed look.

Do you offer weather-resistant or UV-protected materials?

Absolutely. We use high-quality marine-grade materials designed to withstand Idaho’s seasonal weather and sun exposure.

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and materials, but most custom covers and upholstery projects are completed within 2–4 weeks.
